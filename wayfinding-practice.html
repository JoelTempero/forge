Online Community

Exploring Wayfinding

Exploring Wayfinding is an online community designed as an introductory resource for those interested in wayfinding ministry and mission.

Each session incorporates facilitated conversations with a host and guest speaker, along with small group reflection and discussion opportunities.

The initiative is designed to foster dialogue about contemporary approaches to church participation and God's work in Aotearoa.

Session Details

Frequency

3rd Tuesday of each month

Time

7:30pm - 8:45pm (NZ time)

Platform

Online via Zoom

The Experience

What You'll Experience

Each gathering is designed to inspire, connect, and equip you for missional engagement.

Facilitated Conversations

Engage with a host and guest speaker who bring diverse perspectives and experiences in missional ministry and church innovation.

Nationwide Connection

Connect with pioneers from across Aotearoa who share your passion for exploring new ways of being church and participating in God's mission.

Small Group Reflection

Break into smaller groups for deeper discussion, reflection, and mutual encouragement as you process what you're learning together.

Practical Insights

Gain practical wisdom and tools for ecclesiastical innovation and missional engagement that you can apply in your own context.

Who Should Join

Is This For You?

Curious Explorers

You're interested in wayfinding ministry and mission but aren't sure where to start.

Pioneer Leaders

You're already engaged in pioneering work and want to connect with others on similar journeys.

Church Leaders

You lead in an established church and want fresh perspectives on mission and innovation.

Mission Enthusiasts

You're passionate about God's mission and want to learn how to participate more effectively.

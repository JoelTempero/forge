The Practice of Wayfinding

Monthly online gatherings for those exploring new ways of being church

Connect with others across Aotearoa who are exploring new ways of being church and participating in God's mission.

What Each Session Includes

  • Facilitated Conversations

    Engaging discussions with a host and guest speaker sharing insights and experiences.

  • Small Group Reflection

    Break into smaller groups for deeper discussion and personal connection.

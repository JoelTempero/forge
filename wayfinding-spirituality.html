Wayfinding Spirituality

Cultivating spiritual practices for those participating in God's mission.

"God is not primarily interested in what you do, but in who you become."

- Dallas Willard

The Heart of Wayfinding Spirituality

Drawing on Dallas Willard's insight that God prioritizes "who you become" over "what you do," Wayfinding Spirituality emphasizes that spiritual practices are essential for developing intimate relationships with Jesus and engaging in God's work globally.

As apprentices of Jesus, we recognise the need for intentional cultivation of daily spiritual rhythms. There is no universal spiritual practice template - different life seasons demand different disciplines. However, silence and solitude serve as universal anchors for any spiritual journey.

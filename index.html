What We Offer

Equipping for Mission

We focus on grassroots work, connecting emerging leaders with experienced practitioners to create new expressions of church.

Pioneer Training

Our spiritual formation course is designed for those sensing a call to create new expressions of church. We develop leaders for incarnational service with a holistic view of life and mission.

Events

Our Coracle gatherings enable pioneers to come together, share their stories, and explore alternative forms of church community. Experience encouragement and inspiration.

Network

Post-course support includes connection to a wider community of pioneers, experienced coaches, and wisdom mentors who can guide your journey in mission.

Prayer

We welcome prayer support from individuals and communities. If you would like to receive prayer points or commit to praying for pioneer ministry, please reach out.

About Us

Who We Are

Forge Aotearoa provides missional training and cultivates missional communities across Aotearoa New Zealand.

We develop leaders for those seeking incarnational service with a holistic view of life and mission. Our focus is on grassroots work - connecting emerging leaders with experienced practitioners. We recognise that "one size does not fit all" when it comes to expressions of church and mission.

Rather than planting churches ourselves, we equip and support those who are called to pioneer new communities of faith in their own contexts.

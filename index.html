The Wayfinding Journey

Wayfinders go beyond the known, and journey on voyages of discovery to new horizons. Guided by stars, swells, and Spirit rather than maps.

Discipleship is like navigating by Love - listening, discerning, and trusting the Spirit's subtle movements. The waka (canoe) is crewed together: a metaphor for communal faith, shared trust, and spiritual journeying.

What We Offer

Equipping for Mission

We focus on grassroots work, connecting emerging leaders with experienced practitioners to create new expressions of church.

Pioneer Training

Our spiritual formation course is designed for those sensing a call to create new expressions of church with a holistic view of life and mission.

Gatherings

Our Coracle gatherings and Haerenga Tapu pilgrimages enable pioneers to come together, share stories, and explore being church community.

Network

Connection to a wider community of pioneers, experienced coaches, and wisdom mentors who can guide your journey in mission.

Prayer

We welcome prayer support from individuals and communities. Rest in God's presence and join the contemplative rhythm of our community.

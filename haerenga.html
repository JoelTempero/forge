A Sacred Journey Together

Haerenga Tapu is a sacred pilgrimage designed for participants to explore God's story and understand missional wayfinding in Aotearoa New Zealand.

"Wayfinders go beyond the known, and journey on voyages of discovery to new horizons."

- The Wayfinding Approach

What to Expect

  • Community building with like-minded pioneers
  • Encouragement and inspiration for your journey
  • Exploration of missional wayfinding
  • Time in a beautiful marae setting

Who Should Attend

  • Pioneer leaders exploring new expressions of church
  • Church planters and missional practitioners
  • Established church leaders seeking fresh vision
  • Anyone invested in God's church and mission
The Journey

Wayfinding in Aotearoa

Drawing on wayfinding principles, this gathering emphasizes self-understanding, community awareness, and environmental consciousness.

Self-Understanding

Discover your own calling and gifts as you explore what God is doing in and through you.

Community Awareness

Learn to listen to and engage with the communities where God has placed you.

Environmental Consciousness

Understand the context and culture of Aotearoa as you discern God's mission here.

Key People

Meet the passionate team helping to equip pioneers across Aotearoa.

Our Team

The People Behind Forge

A diverse team of practitioners, pastors, and pioneers committed to developing missional leaders.

DT

Darryl Tempero

Minister, Kiwi Church Network

Darryl is minister of "Kiwi Church," a network of small relational communities. He supports the training of ministers and leaders in the wider church. He lives in Christchurch with Michele and their sons and enjoys sport, movies, outdoor activities, and Star Trek.

AO

Anne Overton

Wayfinding Leadership & Community

Anne is passionate about wayfinding leadership and community listening. She led the Community Relations Team for Presbyterian Support Northern and has been involved in community-led initiatives addressing gardens, food security, and social justice.

JO

Josh Olds

Community Minister, The Village Church

Josh is a community minister at The Village Church in Christchurch. He leads an emerging mission initiative with a community drop-in space and serves with Prestons New Mission Seedling. He lives with his wife Susan, three daughters, and their cat Alfie, enjoying sports and outdoor adventures.

SS

Stuart Simpson

Mission Catalyst, Presbytery Central

Stuart is Mission Catalyst for Presbytery Central (lower North Island), supporting congregations in discerning God's mission participation. He lives in Wellington with his wife Lala and three children, two cats, and is a trail running enthusiast.

DG

Diane Gilliam-Weeks

Spiritual Director & Supervisor

Diane is a Spiritual Director and Professional Supervisor in Wellington. She spent twelve years in parish ministry in Wanaka where she pioneered Spirit of the Lake cafe gatherings. A former TVNZ producer/director and Presbyterian Church Communication Director, she was ordained to ministry in 2002.

AT

Abi Trevathan

Kairuruku Whanake, Alpine Presbytery

Abi serves as Kairuruku Whanake (Formation and Development Coordinator) for Alpine Presbytery. She is pursuing a Graduate Diploma in Theology with an intercultural church focus. She lives near Christchurch with Isaac, three children, two cats, and five chickens.

HT

Honey Thrupp

Team Member

Honey is a valued member of the Forge Aotearoa team, contributing to our mission of equipping pioneers across Aotearoa.
